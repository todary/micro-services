      • Fern Grove Cottages
        $$ Hotels, Bed & Breakfast
        16650 River Rd
        Guerneville, CA 95446
        8/16/2015

        We have stayed at the Fern Grove Cottages twice now, and have been incredibly pleased with the experience both times. They are located within walking distance of downtown Guerneville and Russian River beaches, which is a huge benefit. They also have their own pool onsite, and it is a great place to spend some time on a hot day. The grounds are meticulously cared for and their flowers are spectacular, especially during the summer. The breakfast is delicious, and the coffee is great (which is very important to me).

        The cottages are cozy and comfortable. We have found the beds to be very comfortable during both of our stays. Each cottage comes with free wifi and its own patio (which is a great place to take your breakfast back to). Above all else, the staff at Fern Grove have been nothing but courteous, generous, and helpful during our stays here. We will definitely be returning sometime soon!

      • V. Sattui Winery
        $$ Wineries
        1111 White Ln
        Saint Helena, CA 94574
        5/27/2013
        • 1 check-in

        Out of all the places we visited in Napa, this was by far the best bargain in terms of tasting. (And they give you a generous discount for checking in on Yelp!) For a few dollars each, we got to taste six or seven different wines, and they also served complimentary fudge. Despite the place being ridiculously crowded, the server was attentive and personable. Many of the wines are moderately priced, which is nice for people on a budget. The only downside is that this place is a massive, crowded, noisy operation on the weekend, so if you're looking for a quiet, intimate experience, this is not the place for you. However, it is great for groups that are trying to have a fun day without breaking the bank!
