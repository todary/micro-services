  • audreyobscura commented on audreyobscura's instructable Sourdough Starter and Bread Recipe3 months ago

    1.I'm wary of freezing the starter. Yeasts dont like extreme cold or extreme heat. Keeping a dough in the freezer is fine, but a starter seems like it would die if left longer than a couple months. Some bakers dry out their starter, I would check with google and figure out a drying technique that you think could work for you.2.I have left a starter in the fridge for 2 months, but I think that may be about the edge of keeping it without feeding it. It was super acidic, and had a thick layer of hooch on top, but i was still able to revive it.

    You can also add citric salt to bread to make it have a sour flavor, but it kind of feels like cheating :P
